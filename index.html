About Somalia

Somalia, located in the Horn of Africa, is known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and long history.

Top Tourist Destinations

Liido Beach

This beautiful beach in Mogadishu is a bustling location, especially on weekends, where locals and the Somali diaspora gather to relax, swim, and play.

Laas Geel

One of the most extraordinary archaeological and historical sites in all of Africa, featuring vibrant rock paintings in red, yellow, white, and orange.

Alula (Caluula)

The northernmost tip of Somalia, where the Indian Ocean meets the Gulf of Aden. Alula was an important trading center and port. In the 19th and early 20th centuries, it served as the capital of the Majeerteen Sultanate, a powerful Somali kingdom.
