About Somalia

Somalia located in the Horn of Africa and its known for stunning beaches and its rich culture and long history

Top Tourist Destinations

Liido Beach
 Laas Geel
 Alula
  1. Liido Beach. This beautifull Beach in Mugadisho is a bustling location, especially on weekends, where locals and the Somali diaspora gather to relax, swim, and play.
  2. Laas Geel. One of the most extraordinary archaeological and historical sites in all of Africa with Vibrant rock paintings in red, yellow, white and orange
  3. Alula. The northernmost tip of Somalia, where the indian Ocean meets the Gulf of Aden. Alula was an important trading center and port. In the 19th and early 20th centuries, it served as the capital of the Majeerteen Sultanate, a powerful Somali kingdom.
